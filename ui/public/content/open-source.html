Our Open Source Philosophy

Three core principles guide everything we build

Transparency builds trust

When teams use Thunderdome for planning poker, sprint retrospectives, and story mapping, they deserve to know exactly how their data is handled and their sessions are secured. Our open source codebase provides complete visibility into our security practices and data handling.

Community drives innovation

Some of our most valuable features—including LDAP authentication and numerous security enhancements—came directly from community contributions. When developers worldwide can contribute bug fixes, security improvements, and new features, everyone benefits.

Freedom of choice matters

We believe teams should have control over their tools and data. Whether you use our hosted service at Thunderdome.dev or deploy your own self-hosted instance, you get the same powerful functionality without vendor lock-in.

How We Stay True to Open Source

Complete Feature Access

The Thunderdome open source project includes all functionality—from core agile planning features like planning poker, sprint retrospectives, and story mapping, to advanced capabilities that enhance team collaboration and workflow integration.

Self-Hosting Gets Everything

Organizations that choose to self-host Thunderdome get access to the complete feature set at no cost. This ensures that companies with strict data hosting requirements or specific configuration needs never face feature limitations.

Community-First Development

We prioritize contributions and feedback from our open source community. Bug reports, feature requests, and pull requests from users help shape our roadmap and ensure Thunderdome serves real-world agile team needs.

Sustainable Open Source

While the complete Thunderdome codebase remains open source and free, we offer premium subscriptions for our hosted cloud service at Thunderdome.dev. This approach allows us to:

Continue investing in open source development for everyone
Provide professional support for hosted service subscribers
Maintain and improve the free hosted service infrastructure
Develop new features that benefit the entire community

Support Through Donations

Community donations also help sustain development and keep Thunderdome accessible to everyone. Every contribution, regardless of size, directly supports ongoing development and infrastructure costs.

The Key Principle

Every feature we develop becomes part of the open source project. Whether you're using our hosted service, running your own instance, or supporting us through donations, you have access to the same powerful functionality.
